Our story

A Nelson kitchen that has been at it for decades

Broccoli Row opened in 1992 and has been cooking vegetarian food, with the odd seafood plate, on Collingwood Street ever since. The menu is made by hand and changes often, so there is usually something new on the counter alongside the salads, slices and cakes people come back for.

It is a small, friendly spot with two cosy indoor rooms and a few tables outside. The coffee is taken seriously, with dairy-free and alternative milks on hand, and the food leans fresh, generous and made from scratch. Walk in for lunch and stay a while.
