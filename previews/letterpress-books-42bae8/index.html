Our story

A quiet corner for collectors and readers

Letterpress Books sits on the balcony level of Gay's Arcade in the centre of Adelaide, a short step from the fountain in Rundle Mall. It is the kind of place you climb the stairs to find, with shelves of secondhand, rare and out of print books waiting to be browsed.

The focus is Australiana, the history, writing and stories of this country, alongside a broader stock of older and collectable titles. If you are after something particular, it is worth a call. The shop keeps its own hours, so phoning ahead before you visit is the surest way to catch it open.
