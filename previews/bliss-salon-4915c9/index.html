Our story

A friendly chair on the southside of Savannah

Bliss Salon sits in Suite E at 355 Commercial Drive, an easy stop on the southside of Savannah. We are a small salon, so you get unhurried time in the chair and a stylist who listens before reaching for the scissors.

Whether you are after a fresh cut, a color refresh or a proper blow out before a night out, we want you to leave feeling like yourself, only a little more put together. We are open Tuesday through Friday, so it is easy to fit a visit around your week.
