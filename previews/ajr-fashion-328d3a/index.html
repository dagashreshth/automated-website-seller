Our story

Made by hand on Emerson Street

AJR Fashion is a women's clothing shop on Emerson Street in Napier, sewing its own linen pieces rather than buying them in. The idea is simple. Easy linen clothing in shapes that suit real bodies and real days, made in small numbers right here.

Linen is a forgiving fabric. It breathes in the Hawke's Bay summer, softens the more you wear it, and only looks better with a few honest creases. Come in for a look, try things on, and let us help you find a piece that feels like you.
